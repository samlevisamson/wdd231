FitLife Club - Website Site Plan

Site Name

FitLife Club

The name "FitLife Club" represents a community focused on fitness and healthy living. It is simple, memorable, and clearly communicates the purpose of promoting an active lifestyle.

Optional Domain: fitlifeclub.com

Site Purpose

The FitLife Club website will serve as a central hub for fitness enthusiasts. It will provide information about workout programs, membership plans, schedules, and trainers. The site will also guide beginners and encourage people to join and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Scenarios

  • What types of fitness programs are available at FitLife Club?
  • How can I join the club and what are the membership fees?
  • Who are the trainers and what are their qualifications?

Color Scheme

The following colors will be used throughout the website:

  • #2c6e49 (Green) – Used for headings and highlights
  • #1D4AB4 (Orange) – Used for buttons and accents
  • #f4f7f6 (Light Gray) – Used as background
  • #333 (Dark Gray) – Used for text

Typography

Roboto – Used for headings (700 weight).

Roboto – Used for body text (400 weight).
