The Family-Centered Gospel of Jesus Christ

Testimony

I testify that families are central to God’s plan. Through temple ordinances, families can be eternal. Jesus Christ strengthens families and helps us overcome the challenges of our day. As we follow Him, pray together, worship together, and make time for one another, our homes can become places of peace, faith, and love.
